HOW TO PLAY

Unwordle is the opposite of Wordle.

You are trying to guess letters that are NOT in the secret word to score points.

Scoring Rules

  • A +2 points — First time you use a letter that is NOT in the secret word
  • A +1 point — Second time you use that same safe letter
  • A 0 points — Third or more time using that safe letter
  • A −2 points — Letter IS in the secret word (BAD)

Gameplay

Each guess must be a real 5‑letter word (validated using the Merriam‑Webster Dictionary API).

You get 6 guesses per round.

Your total score is the sum of all letter scores across all guesses.

Modes

Daily Mode: One shared secret word per day.

Endless Mode: Unlimited rounds — try to get the highest score.
