HOW TO PLAY

Guess the hidden 5‑letter word in a limited number of tries.

Tile colors show feedback:

  • A Correct letter, correct spot
  • A Letter is in the word, wrong spot
  • A Letter is not in the word
  • A Letter used 3 or more times — counts for 0 points

Daily Mode: one shared word per day.

Endless Mode: unlimited rounds, score as high as possible.

All guesses are validated using the Merriam‑Webster Dictionary API.
